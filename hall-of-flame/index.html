⚡ How XP Works

  • 800 XP = full 10% Engagement Score. Below 800, your grade fraction scales linearly (e.g., 400 XP = 5%, 200 XP = 2.5%). Updated 2026-05-04 from a 400 XP target after many of you breezed past it.
  • Your Jackpot Bell streak is your biggest daily XP source. Streak of 5 = 5 XP per correct answer = up to 15 XP per class.
  • Retrieval Duels earn 1-3 XP per round. Wager rounds can lose XP — risk vs. reward.
  • Boss Battles earn 5-50 team XP depending on performance. Team of the Round gets +20.
  • Volunteer Shares earn +1 XP when your team shares an answer with the class.
  • AI Error Log (50 XP, once per Level), Reflections (10 XP each), and Surveys (10 XP each) add up.
  • Game events throughout each Level offer bonus XP for in-class activities.
  • Your 🛡️ shield absorbs one Jackpot Bell miss per Level. After that, a miss halves your streak.
