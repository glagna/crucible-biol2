🔥 How Streaks Work

  • Show up and complete the Jackpot Bell to start your streak at 1. Each consecutive class adds 1.
  • Your streak IS your XP multiplier. Streak of 3 = 3 XP per correct answer. Caps at 5 XP/answer (15 XP/class).
  • Your 🛡️ shield absorbs one miss. Streak and XP stay intact. You get one shield per Level (3 across the quarter).
  • Shield spent 💨 and you miss again? Your streak drops by half (rounded down). A streak of 10 becomes 5. Painful, but you are still in the game.
  • Badge tiers (Kindling → Phoenix) have no cap. The XP tops at 5, but the streak number keeps climbing. That is your legacy.
